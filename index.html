We are building a simple, reward-based recovery support service. If someone finds your item, we help verify ownership and assist in safe return.

How It Works

1. Report Lost Item

Share details of your lost item through our simple form.

2. Finder Verification

If someone reports finding it, we verify identity before proceeding.

3. Reward Held Safely

If a reward is offered, it is handled carefully and transparently.

4. Item Returned

We coordinate safe return to the rightful owner.

What We Are Building

This is an early-stage local project starting in Patna, Bihar.

  • We manually verify lost and found reports.
  • We do not sell, buy, auction, or store lost items.
  • We only facilitate communication and safe return.
  • Reward is optional and decided by the owner.

We act only as a facilitator between the owner and the finder. We do not guarantee recovery of any item.
